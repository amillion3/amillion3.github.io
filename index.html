My name is Andy Million and I am an aspiring full-stack web developer. I have a strong interest in mapping and related data. During the past 10 years, I have been working with geospatial technologies, from a non-profit to a Department of Defense contractor.

I was first turned onto web development while trying to make a custom web map using Leaflet. It was so satisfying to have a working product and I enjoyed it much more than I thought I would. My heavy familiarity with technology and attention to detail are very helpful solving complex problems in web development.

In my spare time, I am the father of a fiercely independent, but amazing little girl. My wife and I enjoy traveling, hiking, and spending time together. I enjoy rock climbing, mountain biking, backpacking, and most outdoor activities. Motorcycle racing, craft beer, music, and technology are also interests of mine.
